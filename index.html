witch_craft

At its core, witch_craft is designed to streamline various activities, making it an invaluable asset for cybersecurity professionals and enthusiasts. The project encompasses a wide array of functionalities, including but not limited to forensic research, Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) operations, scanning, backup and copying tasks, as well as intrusion testing of applications and APIs.

By choosing Rust as the programming language, witch_craft not only ensures efficiency in terms of performance but also emphasizes security. The language's focus on memory safety and zero-cost abstractions aligns perfectly with the demands of cybersecurity tasks, providing a stable and secure foundation for the project.
